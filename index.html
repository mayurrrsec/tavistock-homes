Welcome

More than a placement.
A place to call home.

We care for young people aged 11 to 18 who live with emotional and behavioural difficulties — often as a result of trauma, loss or unsettled early years.

Many of the young people who come to us have experienced abuse, difficult family circumstances or a series of placements that broke down. Here, they find something different: consistency, patience, and adults who genuinely believe in them.

Our approach combines exceptional one-to-one care with tailored therapeutic support and close partnership with the professionals around each child — a wrap-around service built entirely around who they are and who they can become.

Our approach

Warm, structured & trauma-informed

We understand that behaviour is communication, and that trusting relationships are the foundation of every positive change. Four principles shape everything we do.

01 — Safety

Safety & stability

Consistent routines, clear boundaries and a calm, predictable home where young people know, every day, that they are safe and cared for.

02 — Belonging

A genuine sense of family

Shared meals, everyday moments and small kindnesses — the ordinary rhythms of family life that help a house feel like home.

03 — Relationships

Trauma-informed care

Nurturing, therapeutic relationships built on empathy, acceptance and unconditional positive regard — meeting each young person where they are.

04 — Aspiration

High hopes for every child

Real encouragement and belief — championing education, independence and each young person's own hopes for the future.

Care & team

Skilled people, around the clock

A stable, experienced staff team provides warm, boundaried care every hour of every day, guided by each young person's individual plan.

How we care

  • A dedicated key worker for every young person
  • Individual care & risk plans, regularly reviewed
  • 1:1 staffing through the day; two staff overnight with waking-night cover
  • Positive behaviour support & de-escalation — never punishment
  • Access to an outcomes-based, BACP-registered therapy programme
  • Close partnership with families, carers and placing authorities

Education & aspirations

Helping young people reach further

We believe education is one of the most powerful routes to a positive future — and we champion every young person's learning and ambitions.

Whether in mainstream school, alternative provision or working towards re-engagement, we support attendance, homework and achievement — and celebrate every success along the way.

  • Support with school, college & EHCP arrangements
  • A quiet study space in every bedroom and help with homework
  • Life skills — cooking, budgeting & growing independence
  • Hobbies, interests and community activities

Safeguarding & quality

Keeping young people safe is everything

Safeguarding sits at the centre of all we do. Our policies, training and daily practice are designed to protect every young person and promote their welfare, dignity and rights.

  • Robust safeguarding & child-protection policies
  • Safer recruitment & enhanced DBS checks
  • Clear risk assessment and management
  • Working openly with placing authorities & Ofsted
  • Listening to young people and acting on their voice
