Welcome

A warm welcome to Tavistock Homes

Tavistock Homes is a residential children's home in Nottingham, providing a safe, secure and nurturing 'family home' environment for young men and women aged 11 to 18 who experience emotional and behavioural difficulties (EBD).

We offer placements for young people who have developed EBD as a result of trauma, abuse, damaging family backgrounds and difficult childhood experiences. Many may have been involved in the criminal justice system, or have experienced a number of placement breakdowns.

Our home engages and supports these young people by combining exceptional 1:1 care with tailored therapy programmes and strong partnership working — with the relevant statutory, community, independent and specialist organisations. Together this delivers a wrap‑around care and support service that meets the holistic needs of each child.

Ours is a child‑ and young‑person‑centred service, where staff hold high aspirations for every young person and work around the clock to build trusting relationships, provide consistent boundaries and help each child grow in confidence, independence and hope.

Our home & neighbourhood

A characterful home in the heart of Nottingham

Set in a quiet, tree‑lined residential street, our home is a spacious and characterful period property that blends timeless charm with modern, high‑quality interiors. Its warm red brick and traditional façade help young people feel they live in a real family home, within an established community.

The location offers the best of both worlds — a calm residential setting with excellent access to the amenities, schools, transport links and green spaces of Nottingham.

Close to everything that matters

  • Local schools, colleges & education providers
  • Bus & tram links — easy to travel across the city

  • Health services & GP surgeries close by
